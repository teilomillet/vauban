# Human Oversight Procedure

Review step: a trained service staff member reviews any response that touches
eligibility, benefits, deadlines, or missing documents.
Override capability: staff can override, correct, reject, or stop assistant
output before it is used in a resident-facing decision workflow.
Escalation trigger: escalate when the assistant appears to decide eligibility,
claims certainty from incomplete records, or conflicts with official policy.
Owner: Public Sector AI Risk Owner.
