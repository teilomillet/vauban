# Incident Response Procedure

Incident scope: misuse, privacy breach, incorrect eligibility advice, failure
to disclose AI origin, and serious incident reports from pilot staff.
Escalation or reporting: staff notify the service lead, risk owner, and
compliance contact; severity determines containment and external reporting.
Contact owner: Public Sector AI Risk Owner.
Evidence retention: prompts, trace IDs, report artifacts, and remediation notes
are retained in the pilot evidence folder.
