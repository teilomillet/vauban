# Transparency Notice

Notice type: human interaction and public-facing public-interest text.
AI or automated origin: residents interact with an AI virtual assistant.
Interaction context: chat and web assistant conversation before staff review.
Not human disclosure: the assistant states that it is not a human caseworker.
Public publication context: public-service information may inform the public
about appointments, documents, and service processes.
Human review or editorial control: public-facing answers are reviewed by human
staff before pilot publication.
Editorial responsibility: the agency keeps editorial responsibility for public
information and official notices.
Audience: residents, service staff, and pilot reviewers.
