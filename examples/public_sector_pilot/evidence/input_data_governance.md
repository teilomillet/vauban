# Input Data Governance Procedure

Input data scope: approved prompts, public service text, staff-authored
summaries, and safe synthetic fixtures for the pilot.
Relevance criteria: inputs must be relevant to appointment preparation,
document checklists, transparency questions, or safe uncertainty handling.
Representativeness validation: reviewers sample prompts across public
information, decision boundaries, incomplete records, and transparency cases.
Owner: Public Sector AI Risk Owner.
